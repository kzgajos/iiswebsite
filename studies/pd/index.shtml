The Study

We are building tools to adapt user interfaces to people's individual abilities. In this study we want to understand how people with Parkinson's Disease control pointing devices (such as mouse, trackball, trackpad, etc) so that we can understand how to build more inclusive interfaces in the future.

Who Is Eligible to Participate in This Study

We are looking for participants who:

  • have been diagnosed with Parkinson's Disease
  • are at least 18 years old
  • use a computer at least a few times a week.

Your Participation

Participation in this study will involve:

  • Completing an eligibility survey with one of our researchers over the phone.
  • Completing two sets of simple clicking tasks. This may take between 15 and 30 minutes per set, depending on your individual circumstances.

Compensation

Participatns will be compensated with a $25 Amazon.com gift certificate upcon completion of the study.
